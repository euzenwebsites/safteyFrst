The Safe Behavioral DNA workplace safety test was developed and corroborated by extensive scientific research aimed at identifying the traits that differentiate people who are injured at work from those who are not. Designing a solid and reliable workplace safety test to allow companies to predict safe behavior.

Safe Behavioral DNA allows you dissect a level headed behavior in a measurable way. Companies that use Safe Behavioral DNA reduce their accident rate successfully. An important Mexican manufacturing company applied the Safe Behavioral DNA test to almost 4 thousand potential employees in its selection processes.

almost 4 thousand applicants were classified according to their score on the SBDNA R global indicator, conforming with the 10 score categories, grouping them into low (1-3), medium (4-6) and high probability of safe behavior ( 7-10). The questionnaire includes questions about work related suffered previously, obtaining a rate of 22% for this group, that is, 22% of the almost 4 thousand candidates evaluated acknowledge having had a previous accident.
